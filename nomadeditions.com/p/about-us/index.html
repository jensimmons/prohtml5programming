About Us

Nomad has re-imagined content for mobile platforms; created the means to exploit it; and demonstrated the ability to deliver it.

Nomad Editions is a highly scalable digital media company that has developed a sophisticated publishing infrastructure applicable to multiple publishing initiatives.

  • A skilled and seasoned editorial team, including editor-in-chief, managing editors, copy editors, designers, and award-winning photo department;
  • A sophisticated content management system (publishing engine) which can produce digital output into many digital formats simultaneously. Our principal platform is Mag+ but we can deliver in HTML5, PDF, etc. (Mag+ gives Nomad Editions superior output on Apple devices and certain Android devices especially the Kindle Fire);
  • A track record of producing over 300 issues of attractive, made for mobile, weekly publications for Nomad’s own account since December 2010.

Nomad can develop attractive, curated original digital content from any source. The company has published its own publications such as Uncorked since 2010 and, working with various partners, Nomad has expanded its product offerings.

  • SmartlinkCRMSM/Custom Magazines: We recruit specialized editors and journalists to create editions for major marketers interested in communicating with, and expanding, their customer bases. Click here to learn more about Nomad’s CRM and Custom programs, or call Marjorie Martay at 212-918-0992 or 971-646-1661 (mobile).
  • Publishing Partner: With traditional publishers, we help create new digital content packages — using their existing assets — that are vastly superior to the commonplace digital replicas. Click here to learn more about Nomad’s Publishing Partner programs, or call Mark Edmiston at 212-918-0992.
