Partnerships

In partnership with with other media companies, Nomad Editions creates new digital media products optimized for mobile that build on existing brands.

Nomad can create entirely new editorial material with a partner, or Nomad can take a partner’s existing editorial assets and create something new — custom-made for the digital environment (not a digital replica). Nomad’s Publishing Partners include print publishers and web sites.

Nomad can provide turnkey services to a partner: editorial, design, photo, technology, advertising and sponsorship marketing, subscription promotion and fulfillment.

Partner publications can be specially built just for the iPad (using Mag+) or can be made to run on all smart devices (HTML5).
