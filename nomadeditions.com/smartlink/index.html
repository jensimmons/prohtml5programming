The Nomad CRM Smartlink/ Custom Magazines

Turnkey Services for Marketers: SmartlinkCRM and Custom Publishing

Nomad Editions can help you create an effective loyalty program with a Nomad title (SmartlinkCRM), or Nomad can create a new, Custom digital magazine for your own specific needs and for the most effective Content Marketing.

Attributes. Effective CRM and Content Marketing programs must have certain attributes:

  • Tangible (or Perceived) Value
  • Audience Continuity
  • “Platform/Techno” Flexibility
  • Executional Simplicity
  • Cost Efficiency
  • Program Accountability

Nomad SmartlinkCRM and Custom programs have all these attributes. Nomad matches exactly the right editors, designers and technology to marketers’ audiences and their needs. Nomad CRM and Custom programs are flexible, allow for regular contact with customers, and take advantage of the new communication tools, especially mobile platforms.

Nomad is currently developing Custom digital titles for an international luxury retailer and an international pharmaceutical company. These Custom digital magazines will launch in the Summer and Fall, respectively.

Why SmartlinkCRM? Why Nomad Custom? Why Now?

↑

Smartlink, using existing Nomad titles, provides a complete CRM solution that takes advantage of the most modern communications technologies, and Nomad Custom titles deliver superior branded content for marketers.

Nomad Custom matches the right creative and technical teams with the right objectives for the client’s brand. Nomad can use the marketer’s existing assets, Nomad can create new material, or there can be a mixture.

Mobile Platforms. Smartlink and Custom allow marketers to use the new mobile platforms — tablets and smartphones — as channels for customer retention and customer relationship management.

  • All Smart Devices. CRM and Custom Magazines can be delivered to all “smart” mobile platforms.
  • Frequent Contact with Customers. Smartlink and Custom digital titles can be delivered Weekly/Biweekly/Monthly to customers, on whatever frequency makes the most sense.
  • Advanced Technology. Most advanced content/platform infrastructure.
  • Skilled Editing and Writing. Nomad has complete editorial, photo, and design services. Nomad can create 100% original material and Nomad can also work with existing assets.
  • Long-Lived Programs. Sustainable + scaleable.
  • Budget-Conscious. Smartlink and Custom programs are affordable and efficient.
  • Accountable Program. The results from Smartlink and Custom programs are measurable.
