Help

Are Nomad Editions available on other devices?

Currently, Nomad Editions' magazines are only available for subscription via iTunes for your iPad. In the next few months, we plan to make them available on other devices.

Can I get my Nomad Editions’ magazines through the Apple Newsstand?

Yes, Nomad Editions magazines are now on the Apple Newsstand.

New subscribers: Simply search in the App Store for the name of the publication you want, download it and subscribe. Or, choose your magazine on this site and click 'subscribe'.

Current subscribers: To transfer your current subscriptions to iTunes Newsstand.

  1. Cancel your current subscription's auto renewal:
    • Open the App Store app on your iPad
    • Scroll down to the bottom of the screen and click ‘Apple ID’, then ‘View Apple ID’
    • Log in
    • Scroll down to ‘Subscriptions’ and click ‘manage’
    • Click the magazine name you want to move to the Newsstand and turn off Auto Renewal
    • Click ‘done’
  2. Re-subscribe on the Newsstand:
    • Open the App Store app
    • Search for your magazine's title
    • Click ‘Install’
    • Open your Newsstand app (from the Home Screen)
    • Click your magazine's cover
    • Re-subscribe

If you would like new issues of your magazines to download automatically, go to Settings, click the Store tab and turn on Automatic Downloads for your Nomad Editions' titles. You’ll receive an alert from the Newsstand when a new issue has been downloaded.

Any questions or problems, please contact us: support@nomadeditions.com

Is the Nomad Editions app compatible with iOS5?

Yes, the latest version of the Nomad Editions app and the new versions of the individual magazine apps all work with iOS5.
